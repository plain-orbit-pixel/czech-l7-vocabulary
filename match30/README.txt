Czech L7 Picture Match - Offline App

WHAT IT INCLUDES
- 30 high-priority Lecture 7 food and drink words
- Picture-to-Czech multiple-choice sessions of 10, 20, or 30 questions
- Picture-to-Czech exact-spelling sessions with built-in Czech accent keys
- Immediate correction, saved best score, and focused retry for missed words
- Study screen with Czech pronunciation
- Offline cache after the first web load

USE ON A WINDOWS PC
1. Open index.html for basic practice.
2. For full offline-app caching, serve this folder from a local web server.

INSTALL ON IPHONE
1. Publish or serve the complete folder over HTTPS.
2. Open index.html in Safari once while online.
3. Tap Share, then Add to Home Screen, and enable Open as Web App.
4. Open it once from the Home Screen. The app and all 30 pictures are cached.

Important: keep index.html, app.js, styles.css, service-worker.js,
manifest.webmanifest, the two icons, and the assets folder together.
